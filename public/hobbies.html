My Hobbies

Computer Hardware

Driven by an unwavering passion for computers, I marveled at their limitless potential from a young age. Linus Tech Tips, a notable YouTuber, fueled my curiosity, inspiring me to stay abreast of cutting-edge computer hardware. Today, I eagerly share my expertise, aiding others in fixing and building their machines, grateful for the ever-evolving world of technology.

Gaming

Playing video games allows me to relieve tension and unwind after a long day of school or job. I'm referring to competitive gaming. What could be better than people from all over the world congregating in one place, being cheered on and applauded by millions of other gamers?

Swimming

As a child, my father's regular trips to the swimming pool ignited a lifelong love for the water. Learning to swim at a tender age, I honed my skills and excelled in the sport over time. Embracing the thrill of competitions, I emerged victorious in several, cherishing the memories of those triumphant moments. I still continue to swim, not competitively but as a fitness hobby.

Cooking

Recently, I've found a newfound joy in cooking. Preparing delectable dishes for my loved ones, and relishing them myself, has become a cherished pastime. Cooking with friends adds to the fun, crafting delightful meals to savor while enjoying a movie together, creating moments of pure delight and togetherness.

Dancing

While I may not be the best dancer, my passion drives me to improve continuously. I embrace the rhythm, learning moves for specific songs and letting the music guide me. As a child, I hesitated to dance, fearing judgment. I'm now an active dancer, dancing without a care in the world and having a good time with friends and family.

Eight-Ball

A few years ago, we stumbled upon a pool table, intrigued but clueless. With curiosity as our guide, we attempted to play. Soon, we played regularly, though I felt like a liability. Determined, I embarked on a training journey, practicing relentlessly. Two weeks later, I emerged a force to reckon with, dominating the game with newfound skill and confidence. Now, I play the game as a leisure activity. As an activity to unwind after a long day with a chilled beverage.

Video Editing

My own favorite activity has always been video editing. I enjoy making hilarious YouTube videos. I also work as a video editor for other YouTube channels. Video editing allows me to express myself creatively.
